What we know so far about [ NAME OF YOUR CREATURE ]? The Viral Bloom, scientifically known as Cindathinoes, is a mesmerizing, ocean creature discovered by underwater diver researchers in the deep oceans of Japan. Belonging to the Anthozoan family, it resembles a vibrant coral with fluid tendrils that pulse and flow in real-time, mimicking the graceful movement of sea anemones. Its colorful form is in constant motion, expanding and retracting like a living organism reacting to its environment. What sets the Viral Bloom apart is its dynamic defense mechanism—when approached by a human, it turns black as a defense.
